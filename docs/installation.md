# Installation

## Download

1. Go to the [latest release](https://github.com/demigodmode/ClipKit/releases/latest)
2. Under **Assets**, click `ClipKit.zip` to download
3. Open your Downloads folder and double-click `ClipKit.zip` to unzip
4. Drag `ClipKit.app` into your **Applications** folder
5. Double-click to launch

!!! note "macOS Gatekeeper"
    If macOS says "ClipKit can't be opened", go to **System Settings > Privacy & Security** and click **Open Anyway**.

## Updating

1. Quit ClipKit if it's running (right-click the dock icon > Quit)
2. Download the new `ClipKit.zip` from [Releases](https://github.com/demigodmode/ClipKit/releases/latest)
3. Unzip and drag `ClipKit.app` to Applications
4. Click **Replace** when prompted
5. Launch the updated app

!!! tip "Your data is safe"
    Pinned items and settings are stored in `~/Library/Application Support/`, not inside the app. Updating or reinstalling ClipKit will **never** delete your saved items.

## Requirements

- macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later

## Permissions

ClipKit may request the following permissions:

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| **Accessibility** | Required for the global keyboard shortcut (Cmd+Shift+V) |

You can grant this in **System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility**.
